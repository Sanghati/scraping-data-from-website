    Anything short of a 5/5 for Smokehouse Deli would be injustice.
    Right from the moment I placed a phonecall to reserve a table, till two days after we left the deli with a happy belly, everything at Smokehouse was an absolutely blissful experience.

    We have been eagerly waiting for an opportunity to visit Smokehouse for a long time now, and what better a way to check this on our foodie bucket-list than on a birthday. It was a surprise lunch planned by me, and both the partner and I were beyond surprised at the experience we had here.

    Ambiance is lovely. They have a smoking zone outside and a non smoking zone inside, and the one who booked my table was kind enough to ask my preference. It could get busy on a Saturday afternoon, so a reservation is recommended.

    Coming to the best part of Smokehouse - the food. Not describing each dish in detail is again injustice. Here you go !
    For starters, we picked a salad - Oak Ash Chevre with california oranges. It is undoubtedly the best salad I've had till date. Luscious oranges, caramelized walnuts, olives, cherry tomatoes, and a really really tangy dressing on the lettuce and basil leaves which formed the base of the salad, seasoned with a liberal dosage of feta cheese. PERFECT ! It was an impeccable melange of flavours. Loved how each constituent added a distinct flavour and texture, yet blending very well. Verdict - 5/5
    For pasta, we naturally picked the Roast garlic plum fettucini, being pesto lovers. The steward who took our order said it was only going to be seasoned with pesto, but offered to convert the whole thing to a pesto based sauce, and we were beyond happy. What resulted is one of the best pesto pastas I've ever had. (I've had it possibly in most restaurants that serve pesto pasta at Bangalore, and yes the one at Smokehouse definitely ties at the no.1 spot). Verdict - 5/5
    Next up was cottage cheese steak and ratatouille, which was really good. Soft cottage cheese and a flavorsome ratatouille. But now, on seeing the menu and figuring that we spent roughly Rs 400 on it, I'm beginning to feel it wasn't worth the money. Verdict - 4/5
    Dessert - Hazelnut mousse flan. When it landed on our table, I was a wee bit disappointed as it looked so tiny, and again seemed a little over priced. But when we took the first bite, we were absolutely blown over. It was extremely rich, and dense, unlike the usual mousse outside. One mousse is more than enough for two people. Decorated with hazelnuts, it made a great end to a perfect meal. Verdict - 5/5

    Pricing is on the higher side, but hey, who wouldn't be happy spending on a special occasion ? Especially if the end result is a guaranteed happiness.
    I usually rate restaurants on service, but here, I'd like to call it hospitality. Smokehouse nails it. Absolutely fantastic way to treat customers, make them comfortable, describe menu options and ask for feedback. The staff are courteous, while giving you your space, and they are knowledgeable about the menu and options, which was impressive.

    Final verdict :
    Food - 5/5
    Ambiance - 5/5
    Value for money - 4/5
    Hospitality - 5/5
    I will surely visit many many more times. Definitely recommended !!!!.

    Smoke House Deli.. Definitely my favorite place for European cuisine.. Have been to this outlet twice..

    I like the look and feel of SDH for its mostly white color look.. And the drawings on the walls makes it look pretty neat.. Compared to the Indiranagar outlet, the ambiance here is probably more sober. They have a pretty big seating area and I guess finding this real estate must have cost them a bomb.

    One thing I really dig is their breads. Those are probably some of the tastiest breads I have had. As a group we have shamelessly asked for 3/4 repeats, but they have courteously responded each time. The small sack they bring the breads in is also pretty fancy.

    I am always confused at SDH on what to order. I love their Chermoula spiced grilled chicken, chicken burger, peri peri chicken (if I remember the name correctly) and also 5 spiced chicken (again not sure of the name). Yea, I have stuck to chicken whenever I have come here. I love the fact that they serve fries with burgers. The fries however unhealthy they maybe is hard to resist. I even go the extent of asking for an extra portion of it!! In short, their food is very tasty and portions are pretty big.

    Desserts is a section I have experimented more thanks to the big groups we go in. Tiramisu is really nice. Creme Brulee was yummy. Molten Chocolate cake is a treat for chocolate lovers. Hazelnut Mousse Flan is magical. Panna Cotta is something I din't really like.

    SDH lunches are always fun and memorable. Looking forward to more visits to this place.
